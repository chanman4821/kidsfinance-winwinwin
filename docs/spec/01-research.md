# Win Win Win — Competitive Landscape Brief
**Prepared:** 2026-04-29 · **Confidence:** 0.86 · **Author:** Market Researcher agent

## TL;DR — The wedge
The market has a **clean blind spot at the intersection of playable tycoon depth + real financial literacy curriculum + free-to-play social design for ages 6-18**. No product today puts kids in the role of *founder/operator* — every fintech app casts them as *allowance recipients*. Every Roblox tycoon has a fake economy with no curriculum scaffolding. Every EdTech wraps thin gamification on top of structured lessons.

**Win Win Win = the first product where ages 6-18 run businesses, negotiate trades, acquire rivals, and invest in simulated markets, inside a Roblox-style F2P social world with cosmetic-only monetization.**

## 6. Top 5 Retention Mechanics

1. **Social co-presence** — Roblox 10-K: friend presence is the #1 session-start trigger. *Implication:* show friend's empires on the world map.
2. **Variable reward / uncertain trade outcome** — Hamari et al. (2014) HICSS: variable-ratio schedules produce highest behavioral persistence. *Implication:* NPC counter-offers should feel like loot-box anticipation.
3. **Identity investment via avatar / business customization** — Roblox 10-K: *"creating a sense of persistent identity"*. Sunk-cost = retention. *Implication:* heavy customization on day 1.
4. **Appointment dynamics (daily reset)** — Prodigy + Roblox tycoons use daily quest resets / login bonuses. *Implication:* venture revenue accrual every real-world hour, max collect window 24h.
5. **Status display to social graph** — Roblox 10-K: avatar/experience visible to friends; Adopt Me pet rarity signals status. *Implication:* visible empire size + leaderboard rank among friends.

## 7. COPPA / Safety Pitfalls

**Regulatory baseline:** COPPA (15 U.S.C. §6501) — verifiable parental consent before collecting data from <13; clear privacy policy; parental access + deletion rights; data minimization; no behavioral ads to <13 without consent. [ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/complying-coppa-frequently-asked-questions]

**Recent FTC Enforcement (24-36 months):**

| Company | Fine | What got them | Win Win Win risk if repeated |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Epic Games (Fortnite)** | ~$275M (Dec 2022, FTC Matter 192-3203) [UNVERIFIED URL] | Collected child data without consent; dark patterns made parent refunds nearly impossible; social features defaulted ON for minors | Any pre-checked social/chat defaults; any IAP without explicit parent confirmation |
| **Microsoft/Xbox** | ~$20M (May 2023) [UNVERIFIED URL] | Collected geolocation + email from <13 during Xbox account creation without consent; retained after deletion | Account creation flow for <13 collecting any field without completed parent consent |
| **Amazon/Alexa Kids** | ~$25M (May 2023) [UNVERIFIED URL] | Retained voice recordings indefinitely; overrode parental deletion | Retaining gameplay/chat/voice beyond active session without disclosed retention policy |
| **TikTok/ByteDance** | Pending (FTC→DOJ referral Aug 2024) | Failure to honor parental deletion; collecting from <13 users; Mixed Audience Service obligations not met | **Mixed-audience design (6-18): MUST have separate data pipelines for <13 vs 13+** |

**Win Win Win design guardrails:**

1. **No chat by default for <13.** Roblox 10-K confirms (Jan 2026): *"mandatory age-check systems in all chat-enabled regions designed to check a user's age prior to accessing chat."*
2. **Parent must complete consent flow BEFORE child account is active.** No data collection until confirmed.
3. **No behavioral advertising for <13.** Brand partnerships = non-targeted sponsored content only (e.g., static franchise unlock).
4. **Hard parental spending cap, no upsell bypass.** Roblox confirms: parents *"limit access to features like chat, control screen time and spending."*
5. **Mixed-audience data architecture from day 1.** 6-12 = COPPA-covered tier (no 3rd-party SDKs, no targeted ads, parental consent required). 13-18 = COPPA-exempt with teen-appropriate disclosures. **Separate pipelines from day 1** — retrofitting is what got Epic + TikTok.
6. **In-game currency must carry "no monetary value" disclaimer.** Roblox 10-K: *"Robux have no monetary value and no authorized market or application outside of our Platform."* Mirror this prominently.
